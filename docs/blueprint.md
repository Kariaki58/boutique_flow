# **App Name**: Boutique Flow

## Core Features:

- Public Storefront & Catalog: A customizable public webpage displaying products with images, categories, pricing, availability, descriptions, and a direct 'Order on WhatsApp' button for customer browsing and order initiation.
- Product Checkout Link Generator: Generate unique, shareable links for individual products, enabling customers to select quantity, choose payment methods (bank transfer, cash), and place orders directly via social media platforms like WhatsApp or Instagram.
- Multi-Cart Point-of-Sale (POS): An in-store system supporting multiple simultaneous customer carts, allowing staff to add/remove items, process cash or bank transfer payments, and efficiently complete sales transactions.
- Integrated Order & Payment Tracking: A centralized dashboard to view, manage, and update the status of all orders (from public storefront, checkout links, and POS), including customer details, items purchased, payment method, and manual payment confirmation (e.g., bank transfer proof).
- Product & Inventory Management: Tools for store owners to add/edit products, upload images (via Supabase Storage), set prices, manage stock quantities with automatic reduction upon sale, and mark products as 'Out of Stock' when inventory runs out.
- AI Product Description Generator Tool: Utilize a generative AI tool to assist store owners in creating compelling and descriptive product descriptions based on key product attributes and category, enhancing marketing efforts with minimal effort.
- Store Configuration & Branding: Settings to customize the public storefront (store name, logo, description) and manage crucial business information such as bank account details and WhatsApp contact number, ensuring consistent branding and easy access for customers.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Light mode, prioritizing readability and a fresh aesthetic. Primary color: A sophisticated, muted berry (#732651) to convey professionalism and fashion sense. Background color: A subtle, near-white shade with a hint of the primary hue (#F9F4F7), ensuring clarity and focus. Accent color: A vibrant, clear red (#D92626) to highlight critical actions and notifications.
- Main font: 'Inter', a grotesque sans-serif. This choice offers a modern, objective, and neutral appearance suitable for both headlines and body text, ensuring excellent readability and a streamlined feel across all devices.
- Utilize simple, clean, and intuitive line icons. Icons should clearly represent their function (e.g., cart, menu, user, share) to minimize learning curve for users of all technical abilities, consistent with a mobile-first PWA approach.
- Layouts are 100% mobile-responsive and optimized for touch-first interaction. Content is organized with clear visual hierarchy, ample white space, and large tap targets, mimicking the fluidity and directness of a native mobile application for both sellers and customers.
- Implement subtle, purposeful animations to provide immediate feedback to user actions, such as item additions, status updates, or page transitions. Animations should be swift and smooth, enhancing the 'app-like' experience without causing delay or distraction.
